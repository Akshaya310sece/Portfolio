Experience

Roots Industries India
Industrial Visit · Internship
2026

Visited the Roots Industries India Private Limited horn manufacturing plant — one of India's leading manufacturers of acoustic warning devices — gaining direct exposure to the complete product lifecycle of electromagnetic and air horns, from raw material processing through precision assembly to quality certification and dispatch.

Observed the plant's electrical systems infrastructure at scale: transformer stations, motor-driven conveyor assemblies, and PLC-controlled automation lines managing high-volume production runs. Developed a sharp understanding of how circuit-level electrical design translates into ruggedized, mass-manufactured industrial components.
